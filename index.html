about us

WHO WE ARE

Simone Coffee is made up of people who like people. As a whole, we want to be kind to you and make you coffee that tastes delicious. We're serious about both things in the way that we spend far too much of our personal time working to become better at what we do. We like to: be outdoors, read books, get involved in our community, make music, make art, make food, eat food, drink delicious things, visit friends' coffee shops, explore the world, and we like to make coffee for Everett and Bellingham! Our lovely barista and kitchen crew are who make the place really interesting. Come in, get to know them, and find yourself in easy conversation.

We promise to serve you in a way that is hospitable, friendly, non-judgmental, and encouraging. We promise to make your drink or food to the best of our abilities. And to make coffee fun again. We're definitely into that.
